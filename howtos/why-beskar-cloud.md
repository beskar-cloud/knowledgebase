# Why Beskar cloud?

Beskar cloud was formed to share expertise how to run and maintain OpenStack cloud on top of Kubernetes.

Beskar cloud community brings
 * proven production OpenStack architecture
 * optimized operations at a reasonable amount of effort.
 * best-practice cloud deployment following [DevOps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DevOps), [IaC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Infrastructure_as_code) and [GitOps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DevOps#GitOps) approaches
 * stabilized and patched [openstack-helm](https://github.com/beskar-cloud/openstack-helm) and [openstack-helm-infra](https://github.com/beskar-cloud/openstack-helm-infra) repositories
on top of existing opens-source repositories.
